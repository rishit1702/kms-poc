Project Volt - EV Battery Management System

Customer: Confidential Tier-1 automotive OEM (referred to internally as "OEM-A").
Engagement: 18-month design + manufacturing partnership signed Feb 2026.

Scope:
- BMS firmware on STM32H7 MCUs
- Cell balancing algorithms (passive + active hybrid)
- CAN-FD communication with vehicle ECU
- Over-the-air update infrastructure
- Functional safety: ISO 26262 ASIL-C

Lead site: Hyderabad. Project director: Anitha Reddy.
Estimated revenue: 8.5 crore INR over engagement period.

Hardware: 96-cell pack, 400V system, NMC chemistry.
First B-sample delivery: August 2026.
