VVDN Technologies Q1 2026 Strategic Roadmap

Executive Summary:
VVDN Technologies is targeting 40% YoY revenue growth in FY2026, driven by expansion in 5G product engineering, EV charging infrastructure, and AI-edge device verticals. The Gurugram facility will scale from 850 to 1,200 engineers by end of Q3 2026.

Key Initiatives:
1. Project Atlas - 5G Open RAN Platform
   - Approved: March 5, 2026
   - Budget: 2 crore INR
   - Lead: Sandeep Mishra (AI Engineering)
   - Target launch: Q4 2026
2. Project Volt - EV Battery Management System
   - Partnership with Tier-1 automotive OEM
   - 18-month engagement
   - Hyderabad center to lead
3. Internal AI Tooling
   - KMS (Knowledge Management System)
   - AI Recruitment Bot for Talent Acquisition

Risks: Talent retention in AI/ML roles, semiconductor supply chain volatility.
