VVDN Internship Program - Policy & Guidelines

Eligibility:
- Penultimate or final-year B.Tech/M.Tech students from approved colleges (PEC, IIT, NIT, IIIT, BITS, etc.)
- Minimum CGPA of 7.0

Duration: 6 months (extendable to 9 with mentor approval).
Stipend: Rs 25,000/month for B.Tech, Rs 35,000/month for M.Tech.

Mentor assignment: Each intern is assigned a primary mentor (typically Senior Engineer or above).
Weekly 1:1 with mentor is mandatory.

Deliverables:
- Mid-term review at month 3
- Final project demo + code review at month 6
- One technical blog post on internal Confluence

PPO (Pre-Placement Offer) criteria:
- Mid-term and final review ratings >= "Meets Expectations"
- Mentor recommendation
- Open headcount in the team

Code of conduct: NDA in effect from day 1. No public disclosure of project details.
