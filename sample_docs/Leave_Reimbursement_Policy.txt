Leave & Reimbursement Policy - VVDN Technologies

Annual Leave: 18 days per calendar year, accrued monthly. Carry-forward up to 9 days.
Casual Leave: 12 days, no carry-forward.
Sick Leave: 7 days, medical certificate required for >2 consecutive days.

Bereavement: 5 days for immediate family.
Marriage: 7 days, once in tenure.

Reimbursements:
- Internet: Rs 1,500/month for hybrid employees
- Phone: Rs 800/month (claim quarterly)
- Travel for project work: economy domestic, Premium Economy international (>6 hr flights)
- Per diem (domestic): Rs 1,800/day
- Per diem (international): USD 80/day Tier-1 cities, USD 60/day others

Submit claims within 30 days of expense. SAP Concur portal.
