AI Project Review Process

Stage gates:
1. Concept (1-page brief, owner: PM or EM)
2. POC (working demo on internal data, owner: tech lead)
3. Pilot (running with 1-2 customers or internal teams, success metrics defined)
4. Production (SLOs, runbooks, on-call rotation)

POC review checklist:
- Does it address a real, validated problem?
- Is the data realistic enough to predict pilot performance?
- Are failure modes understood?
- What's the cost-per-query at projected scale?

Reviewers:
- POC: EM + 1 senior engineer
- Pilot: EM + Director + Customer success rep
- Production: VP Engineering + Security + SRE

Cadence: Reviews scheduled bi-weekly on Thursdays. Calendar: ai-reviews@vvdntech.in.
