Customer Demo Etiquette - Field Engineering

Pre-demo:
- Dry run end-to-end at least 24h before. Include network failure scenarios.
- Pre-load demo data and warm up models. First-call latency should not be the customer's experience.
- Have a fallback: pre-recorded video, screenshots, backup slide deck.

During demo:
- Lead with outcomes ("here's the answer the system returns") before drilling into architecture.
- When showing failure modes, frame as design choices ("system refuses when context is insufficient, by design - prevents hallucination").
- If something breaks: don't panic, narrate ("interesting, let me show you what's happening here"). Then fall back.

After demo:
- Send summary email within 4 hours, including recording link, slides, and proposed next steps.
- Capture customer questions and unknowns - log them in HubSpot.
